Hello

Bakertunes is the music composed and arranged by Sarah Baker, composer-in-residence for Services for Education, Music Services.

I compose and arrange songs for primary and secondary education, some of which is published by Starshine Music and by Edgy Productions.

I am also a private piano teacher. I compose pieces and studies for people learning piano and working towards ABRSM grade exams, many of which are available to download for free in the Piano Music section of this site.
